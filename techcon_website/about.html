Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of software engineers and entrepreneurs. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most influential technology conferences globally, attracting over 10,000 attendees annually. From breakthroughs in AI to grassroots coding bootcamps, TechCon continues to evolve with the world of innovation.

Our Mission

At TechCon, we strive to foster innovation, promote inclusive tech communities, and provide a platform where creativity meets practicality. We believe in the power of collaboration and open access to knowledge to create a more connected, empowered, and sustainable future.

Past Speakers

TechCon has proudly hosted some of the world’s most forward-thinking minds in technology. Here are a few of our past speakers:

Dr. Ada Chen

Cybersecurity expert and Harvard professor, Ada has led groundbreaking research in AI-driven defense systems. She spoke on “Ethical Algorithms” at TechCon 2022.

Jamal Rodriguez

A trailblazer in blockchain development, Jamal has built decentralized finance platforms used by millions. His 2021 talk focused on Web3’s global potential.

Sofia Mensah

Sofia is a social entrepreneur who founded Code4Hope, bringing coding education to underserved communities across Africa. Her talk in 2023 was titled “Tech for Good.”
